    • Welcome to the Purple Line Corridor Coalition Website

    To ensure equitable and sustainable development along the Purple Line corridor, the Purple Line Corridor Coalition was founded in 2012. One major work of the PLCC is the Purple Line Community Compact, which a commitment by its signatories to work together to achieve a long-term vision for a prosperous, equitable, and environmentally sustainable Purple Line corridor. This Purple Line Dashboard is created to monitor major goals set in the Community Compact. The Dashboard includes five parts, the highlight page, local business, labor market, housing choices, and neighborhood.
